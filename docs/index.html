About


Welcome to Principles of Spatial Analysis, one of the four core modules for the MSc in Geographic and Social Data Science here at UCL Geography. This handbook contains practical material for four weeks of this year’s module.

Moodle

Moodle is the central point of your learning experience for GEOG0114 and contains everything you need to know about it. It’s where you’ll find links to all lecture content, reading materials, planned activities as well as key module and assessment information
